Who we are

NetEquity is an infrastructure sharing start-up based in San Francisco, CA. We believe infrastructure sharing can make Internet for all financially feasible.

Our mission is to eliminate bandwidth disparity by making fiber infrastructure open access and pervasive. We have established strategic partnerships with mission driven organizations and define success as achieving 100% broadband penetration globally.

Purpose

Level the playing field for human aspiration.

Mission

Eliminate bandwidth disparity by making open access fiber pervasive.

Strategy

north star

03. / solution

What we do

NetEquity assumes early stage project risk, assembles a feasible deal, deploys an open access fiber network and creates a bandwidth marketplace.

1. Development stage
(~2 Years)
  1. Sign NDA with utility
  2. Obtain grid data
  3. Prepare investor memo
  4. Obtain capital commitments
  5. Win the bid in a competitive tender
2. Financial Close
(~6 mothhs)
  1. Anchor Tenant agreement with utility
  2. Customer agreements
  3. Fiber supply contract
  4. SDN Vendor selection
  5. Management onboarding
  6. Debt + Equity Financing
3. Deployment + Testing
(~6 mothhs)
  1. Provide oversight during deployment
  2. Launch user portal
  3. Finalize impact measurement metrics
4. Full Operation
(20 years)
  1. Implement governance to ensure open access
  2. Maintain network oversight
  3. Create partnerships
  4. Evangelize broadbrand
