05. / principles

Our principles

The NetEquity financing model treats fiber like a utility and is based on the following 10 principles:

1. Open access: The deployed fiber network will be equally accessible to all customers and will be prohibited from entering into exclusive contracts.

2. Wholesale only: The fiber network will provide wholesale bandwidth services only and will be prohibited from offering retail services.

3. Lit service: Bandwidth will be provided as a lit-service to endpoints.

4. Capped returns: The equity owners of fiber network will be entitled to a fixed or formula driven return subject to performance benchmarks.

5. Benefit sharing: Financial benefits arising from more endpoints connected will be shared with all stakeholders.

6. Rural first: The fiber network will be incentivized to execute on a rural-first strategy.

7. Local ownership: Financing schemes will be created for local managers to achieve majority ownership of the fiber network.

8. Connectivity as KPI: Management team’s bonus will be tied to number of endpoints connected and fiber penetration in rural areas.

9. Network transparency: Fiber pop locations and fiber maps to be made publicly available.

10. Uniform and transparent: Pricing per endpoint will be uniform for all.
